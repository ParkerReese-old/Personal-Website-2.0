Can you believe I was using images that were multiple megabytes each! RoboSport In my third year engineering course at U of S we had to make a game called Robo Sport. It was a simple board game that was to be implemented with Java. The point of the class wasn't necessarily to make an awesome game, but rather to learn the processes of software development. We had meetings, pair programming sessions, brainstorming sessions, the whole works. I must say I quite enjoyed working amongst our small team of four. Team Soccer For no particular reason I decided to try and make a game called Team Soccer. The objective is simple, get the ball into the other teams net. But! You and your partner are tied together by a rope. I got the game fully functional for one level and four players, but have since moved on to bigger and better things. Game Jam: Killer Bunny I decided to do a Game Jam with a few people at U of S. We had 48hrs to whip together as much of a game as we could. The theme for this Game Jam was "You are the Villian". With that, we decided to make a game around being the evil white bunny from Monty Python and the Holy Grail. You spent the game hunting down knights in a dark cave and, well, killing them. Game Jam: Desmon The Moon Bear This little gem is the outcome of my first ever Game Jam and first ever crack at Unity.
